Psychology

I am currently a second year psychology major at UCR. I took psychology courses to fulfill my social sciences requirement, however, I fell in love with psychology. There are many different types of psychology but I am interested in the psychology of child development. I am studying to hopefully apply to graduate school for occupational therapy. I am trying to become an occupational therapist as they mainly work with children as young as 1 to 18 years of age. I know there are many kids with learning disabilities and some with mental disabilities and would like to support them in all their future endeavors.

I have been working with the Boys and Girls Club of Redlands as a Youth Development Specialist for 3 months. I am there to support students in the Riverside School District whether it be with homework or with physical activities. My daily work schedule consists with homework tutoring, some outdoor time and then doing some art or teaching them a new skill that they could use.

My goal in life is to be able to give back to my parents. I want to be able to buy them a house and take care of them as they grow older. My parents have given up a life in Vietnam to immigrant to America for a better future for my sister and I. They work 7 days a week from 9am to 8pm to pay for bills and provide food everyday. I know what I can do for them will never be able to amount to how much they provide to me but I want to do my best.
